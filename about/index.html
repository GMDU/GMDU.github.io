About GMDU

GMDU, or Gears (and) Moxvallix Datapack Universe, is a small team of datapack developers making needlessly complicated datapacks for Minecraft: Java Edition. The GMDU team, unsurprisingly, consists of Gears, and Moxvallix.

Our main focus has been Code of Copper, but we are looking to work on some other projects as well. All of our projects can be found on our Projects Page.
